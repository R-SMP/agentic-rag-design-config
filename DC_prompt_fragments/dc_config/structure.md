The propeller consists of:
1. An outer ring characterised by its radius, height, and wall thickness.
2. Blades, each divided into three radial sections:
   - Inner section: where the blade meets the central rotating shaft.
   - Middle section: between inner and outer; its radial position is a design
     parameter and need not be the geometric midpoint.
   - Outer section: the blade tip, furthest from the centre.
